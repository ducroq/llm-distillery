# Sustainability Pillar 3: Policy Works Filter

**Version**: 1.0
**Pillar**: "Policy Works (when done right)"
**Purpose**: Prove smart policy drives change - show blueprints others can copy

## Overview
Evaluates climate policies for measurable outcomes and replicability. Filters out announcements without data.

## Key Dimensions (see prompt for details)
1. Policy Outcomes (30%) - Measurable before/after change
2. Replicability (25%) - Can others copy?
3. Political Durability (15%) - Survives government change?
4. Speed of Impact (10%) - How fast?
5. Equity (8%) - Just transition?
6. Enforcement (7%) - Teeth?
7. Unintended Consequences (3%)
8. Policy Spreading (2%) - Others adopting?

## Examples
- **Norway EV Policy (9.1)**: 90% market share, 15+ countries copying
- **EU ETS (7.5)**: Emissions fell, strong enforcement, some equity issues
- **Net-zero pledge (2.0)**: No outcomes yet, no enforcement

Part of 5-pillar framework with tech_deployment, economic_viability, nature_recovery, movement_growth.
